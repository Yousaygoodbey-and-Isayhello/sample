My accidental entry to the drummer's world

I have been playing synths and piano all my life. I picked up mu bass guitar when I was 18 and that was it until I was 30 when I realize the benefit of drumming.

The song that made me

One day I went to the Earth, Wind & Fire concert in Chicago. It was an ordinary day. When the show was over and came home, I was drinking a bit with my friend and I picked up a pair of drumsticks and started playing. It surprised my friends and myself I decided to take drumming seriously.

Here is the benefit of playing drums:
  • It's meditating
  • music sheet is not as hard to read
  • Hands and eye coordination
